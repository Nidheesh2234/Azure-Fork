### Method 1: Click on Song Cards

### Method 2: Use the Music Player
1. After selecting a song, you'll see it in the bottom music player
2. Click the **white play button** in the center to open the YouTube player modal
3. In the modal, click the big red **"Play"** button to start the embedded YouTube video

### Method 3: Open in YouTube Directly
1. In the player modal, click **"YouTube"** button to open the song in a new YouTube tab
2. This gives you full YouTube controls and the complete video

## Adding New Songs

1. Go to **"Your Library"** tab
2. Click the red **"Add YouTube Song"** button
3. Fill in the form:
   - Paste YouTube URL
   - Enter song title and artist
   - Select category (Bhajans, Keerthanams, etc.)
   - Choose language (Telugu, Malayalam, Tamil, Sanskrit)
4. Click **"Add Song"** to add it to your collection
